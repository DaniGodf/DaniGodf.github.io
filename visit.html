Get ready for pure joy! Our baby goat cuddling session are the perfect mix of heart-melting cuteness and relaxing country charm. Farm Hayride Tour

Climb aboard our classic hay wagon for a scenic, relaxing ride through the heart of the farm! This guided tour takes you across rolling pastures, vibrant fields, and peaceful wooded trails, giving you a behind-the-scenes look at daily life on the farm. You'll meet some of our animals along the way, learn fun facts about our crops and sustainable practices, and soak in the fresh air and countryside views. It is the perfect way to experience the rhythm of farm life — slow, simple, and full of charm. Great for families, couples, school groups, or anyone who just wants a taste of the good life.

Wine Tasting

Join us for a delightful afternoon of wine tasting at Bubcat Farms! Enjoy the beautiful farm views as you sip and savor — it's a perfect way to unwind and connect with nature. Sample a selection of our finest wines, each crafted with care from our own grapes. Our knowledgeable staff will guide you through the tasting process, sharing the stories behind each bottle and the unique flavors that make our wines special. Whether you're a seasoned wine enthusiast or just starting to explore the world of wine, this experience is sure to please your palate.
