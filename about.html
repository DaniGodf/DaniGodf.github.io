The heart of Bubcat Farms began beating over 70 years ago, when two young calfs, Daniel and Catherine, first tilled the soil with little more than a dream. Nestled in a quiet valley, the farm started with a few cows, rows of corn, and a deep love for the land. Over the decades, it’s grown — but it’s never lost its roots.

Our Mission:

At Bubcat Farms, our mission is to cultivate wholesome, sustainable food while honoring the land, supporting our community, and preserving the farming traditions passed down through generations. We are committed to ethical practices, environmental stewardship, and producing high-quality products that nourish both body and spirit.

Meet Our Animals!

Chelsea and Daisy

Chelsea is our oldest animal on the farm at 20 years old. She is the sweetest cow you will ever meet and loves to be pet and brushed. She is the mother of our newest calf, Daisy.

Chimney

Chimney is our shyest friend. He loves to explore and will eat to his heart's delight so make sure to always have food on hand when you're around him.

Pearl

Pearl is quite the independent woman but also a jokster, so keep a cautious eye around her.

Percy

Percy is the most caring animal you will ever meet. Always understanding of others and never makes a nuisance of himself.

Quack

Quack is a clumsy one but lives a happy life walllowing in mud. Always have a ball around you because he loves fun games.
