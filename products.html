From pasture-raised livestock to naturally grown product, everything we cultivate is rooted in respect — for the soil, the animals, and the people we feed. We steer clear of synthetic chemicals and GMOs, focusing instead on traditional methods, seasoned rhythms, and a whole lot of hard work. It's farming the way it was meant to be — simple, honest, and full of heart.

Red Pontiac Potatoes

Bright red skin, creamy white flesh, and a rich, hearty flavor — our Red Pontiac Potatoes are gown with care right here at Bubcat Farms. Perfect for boiling, roasting, and hearty Southern dishes, these potatoes thrive in Tennessee's soil and sunshine. Hand-planted, hand-harvested, and full of real farm goodness.

Soybeans

At Bubcat Farms, we grow Progeny 4818RX soybeans — a variety bred for the Southern climate and known for strong yields, healthy plants, and excellent standability. Our Tennessee soil and careful farming practices produce soybeans that are nutrient-dense, versatile, and dependable for food production, livestock feed, or replanting.

Orchard Collection

From sweet and crisp to tart and tangy, Bubcat Farms proudly offers four signature apple varieties, all grown with care right here in the heart of Tennessee. Our Honeycrisp, Gala, Pink Lady, Granny Smith, and Enterprise apples each bring a unique flavor, color, and crunch — perfect for snacking, baking, and everything in between.

Berry Collection

Grown under sunny Southern skies with care and sustainable methods, our Strawberries, Blueberries, Raspberries, and Blackberries are picked at their peak for maximum flavor. Whether you’re whipping up a pie, making jam, or simply snacking, our berries deliver farm-fresh goodness in every bite.

Sweet Corn

There's nothing like fresh, locally grown sweet corn from Bubcat Farms. Our sweet corn varieties — Silver Queen, Butterfly, and Peaches & Cream — are handpicked at the peak of ripeness, ensuring every ear is packed with natural sweetness and tender kernels. Grown in the warm Tennessee soil, our corn is the perfect summer treat for grilling, boiling, or enjoying straight from the cob.

Tomato Varieties

From juicy, slicable varieties to rich, flavorful heirlooms, Bubcat Farms offers a tomato for every taste. Our Better Boy, Celebrity, Brandywine, and Roma tomatoes are grown in Tennessee’s fertile soil, harvested at peak ripeness, and perfect for everything from fresh salads to homemade sauces. Whether you're grilling, slicing, or snacking, these farm-fresh tomatoes deliver vibrant, full flavor straight to your table.

Zucchini

Bubcat Farms grows a variety of zucchini perfect for Tennessee’s growing season — from Black Beauty, the classic dark green zucchini, to the uniquely striped Cocozelle and the delicate yellow-and-green Zephyr. Whether you're grilling, sautéing, or adding to your favorite dishes, our zucchini is tender, flavorful, and packed with nutrients. Make sure to stop by our shop to grab yourself a loaf of our homemade zucchini bread!

Wine Grapes

At Bubcat Farms, we grow only the finest grapes for winemaking. Our Chardonnay, Cabernet Sauvignon, Cynthiana, Vignoles, and Muscadine grapes are hand-picked at peak ripeness and crafted for superior wines. These varieties thrive in Tennessee's climate, and are sustainably grown and nurtured with care. Our grapes are the foundation of wines that reflect the land and the tradition of Tennessee winemaking.
